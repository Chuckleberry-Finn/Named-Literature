description=Applied to: Books, Magazines, HottieZs, TVMagazines, ComicBooks, Newspaper, Wordsearches, Crosswords.
description=
description=[list]
description=[*] Titles are applied randomly to books from a list of over 3000+ titles.
description=
description=[*] The 3000+ titles are sorted by year/decade/century written, with a preference for modern (1993); include every century up to the 4th.
description=
description=[*] Book descriptions and tooltips will also include author and publication date for extra flavor (text).
description=
description=[*] Magazines utilize a similar system but just of magazine titles from a list of 60+ titles.
description=
description=[*][B]Additional Features:[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] Literature items will also still stack as their default name (example: "book"). (This is done by editing [b]*[/b][i]two functions of the inventory pane[/i].)
description=[*] Literature items can be re-read for a diminished stress/boredom/unhappiness relief. (If given enough time you can re-read for full amount.)
description=[*] Literature items are color coded (based on a seeded process), colors should always be consistent. [b](Coloring 3D models not yet working/may not be possible.)[/b]
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]*[/b]Please report any issues especially with items stacking strangely.
