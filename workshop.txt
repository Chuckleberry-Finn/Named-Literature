description=[h3][b]This mod does not use item scripts. Everything is lua based.[/b][/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*] Titles are applied randomly to books from a list of over 3000+ titles.
description=
description=[*] The 3000+ titles are sorted by year/decade/century written,
description=with a preference for modern (1993); include every century up to the 4th.
description=
description=[*] Book descriptions and tooltips will also include author and
description=publication date for extra flavor (text).
description=
description=[*][B]Additional Features:[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] Books will also still stack as "book". (This is done by editing [b]*[/b][i]two functions of the inventory pane[/i].)
description=[*] Books can be re-read for a diminished stress/boredom/unhappiness relief. (If given enough time you can re-read for full amount.)
description=[*] Books are color coded (based on a seeded process), colors should always be consistent. [b](Coloring 3D models not yet working/may not be possible.)[/b]
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]*[/b]Please report any issues especially with items stacking strangely.
